Lasanga Recipe

Lasanga is a one of those classic Italain dishes that combines pasta and meat into a great meal.

Ingredents

  • Sweet Italian sausage adn lean ground beef
  • One onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dired basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasanga noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover the foil and bake
